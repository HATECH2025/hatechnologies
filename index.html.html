About H & A Technologies

A Pinnacle of Science - Your Trusted Partner Since 2020

Who We Are

H & A Technologies stands as a beacon of excellence in the analytical instrumentation industry. For over a decade, we have been the trusted partner for laboratories, research institutions, and industrial facilities worldwide, providing cutting-edge analytical instruments and laboratory consumables that drive scientific discovery and innovation.

Founded on the principles of precision, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we specialize in offering technologically superior solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern laboratories. Our comprehensive portfolio spans thermal analysis instruments, material characterization equipment, and essential laboratory consumables.

Our Legacy

Many instruments deployed globally and a precision accuracy rate of 99.9%, we have established ourselves as leaders in analytical excellence. Our partnership with the Edward Orton Jr. Ceramic Foundation, with its 125+ years of heritage, further strengthens our commitment to providing world-class thermal analysis solutions.

Thermal Analyzers

Orton Ceramic Foundation Products

DSC/DTA Systems

Differential Scanning Calorimetry

  • Temperature Range: -150°C to 750°C
  • Heating Rate: 0.1 to 200°C/min
  • Sensitivity: < 0.2 μW
  • Sample Size: 0.1 to 50 mg

High-precision thermal analysis for measuring heat flow and temperature transitions in materials.

TGA Systems

Thermogravimetric Analysis

  • Balance Sensitivity: 0.1 μg
  • Temperature Range: RT to 1500°C
  • Weight Capacity: 1g maximum
  • Atmosphere Control: Inert/Oxidizing

Precise measurement of weight changes in materials as a function of temperature or time.

Orton Dilatometer Series

Precision thermal expansion measurement instruments

  • Temperature Range: -190 to 1600°C
  • Resolution: 0.1 μm
  • Sample Length: 12-16 inch zones
  • Applications: Ceramics, Glass, Refractories

Essential for determining coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) of ceramics, glass, and advanced materials.

Glass Viscometer Systems

High-temperature viscosity measurement

  • Temperature Range: RT to 1700°C
  • Viscosity Range: 10^8 to 10^15 Poise
  • Test Methods: Fiber Elongation, Rod
  • Standards: ASTM C338, C336

Determine softening point, annealing point, strain point, and viscosity characteristics of glass compositions.

STA Systems

Simultaneous Thermal Analysis

  • DSC Sensitivity: < 1 μW
  • TGA Resolution: 0.1 μg
  • Temperature Range: RT to 1600°C
  • Sample Size: 0.1 to 900 mg

Combined DSC and TGA measurements in a single instrument for comprehensive thermal analysis.

Gradient Furnace Systems

Advanced temperature gradient analysis

  • Temperature Range: 500°C to 1400°C
  • Gradient Length: 300mm
  • Temperature Accuracy: ±2°C
  • Applications: Liquidus, Devitrification

For glass crystal formation analysis and liquidus temperature determination.
